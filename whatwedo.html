What we do

COVID-19

Information and guidance relating to the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.

Funding and Innovation

Investment from Europe, and funding available for those in business, housing, sport and elsewhere.

Arts and culture

London is renowned for its creativity, arts and culture. We work to strengthen our position as a capital of culture.

Business and economy

We make London the strongest it can be to attract and support small, medium and large businesses.

Communities

Find out more about what education and training is available.

Education and youth

London Curriculum, school initiatives, preparing young people for work, family support and improving school standards.

Environment

London’s environment needs to be maintained and protected and its resources used more efficiently.

Health

London can and should be a place where everyone is able to lead a healthy and fulfilling life.

Fire and Resilience

Find out about the work of the London Fire Brigade and the Resilience team at the GLA.

Housing and land

We're increasing the amount of affordable homes in London and releasing new land for housing.

Policing and crime

The Mayor's Office for Policing And Crime (MOPAC) provides information on our crime priorities and police performance.

Planning

We prepare the Mayor’s London Plan and planning guidance. We also advise on local plans and key planning decisions.

Regeneration

We are improving London's people, places and prosperity, to help the Mayor achieve Good Growth.

Jobs and Skills

Helping Londoners get the skills they need to take advantage of new opportunities for employment and lifelong learning.

City research & analysis

The evidence, analysis and data which allows City Hall to form policy and strategy for London.

Sports

We fund grassroots activity across the capital and celebrate London as a leading destination for major sporting events.

Transport

London's transport system needs to meet the demands of a growing city. See how we're modernising the transport network.

Volunteering

The Mayor's volunteering and social action team which helps Londoners give back to our communities.
